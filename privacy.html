Setlist Streamer – Privacy Policy

Effective: October 23, 2025

Setlist Streamer is a simple, read‑only Progressive Web App (PWA) that helps you browse and play publicly available concert recordings from archive.org. We don’t run user accounts, we don’t collect personal information, and we don’t use ads or analytics SDKs.

What data we collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number, payment details, or location.

Local storage (on your device)

To remember things like your favorites, filters, or recent activity, the app may store small, anonymous settings on your device using browser storage (e.g., localStorage or IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device unless you back it up yourself. You can clear it anytime from your browser or app settings.

Network requests to third‑party services

The app fetches public content from archive.org to display recordings and metadata. While we don’t collect personal data, your device makes standard web requests to those services; those services may receive typical technical information (e.g., IP address, user‑agent) needed to deliver content. Please review their policies for details.

App permissions

The app does not request access to sensitive device permissions (e.g., contacts, camera, microphone, photos, precise location).

Children’s privacy

Setlist Streamer is a general‑audience app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data security

Because we don’t run accounts or collect personal data, we don’t maintain user data servers. Any settings saved on your device remain there until you clear them.

Data retention & deletion

We don’t retain personal data on our servers. Any local settings saved on your device can be removed by clearing the app/site data in your browser or mobile OS settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy over time. We’ll post the updated version at the same URL and change the “Effective” date above.
